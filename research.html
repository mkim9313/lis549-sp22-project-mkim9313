Pack With Us

Research

Interview

Our team conducted semi-structure interviews with a TSA agent and three frequent and non-frequent travelers. Each interview was around 60 minutes with a set of questions to find common emotions and actions flyers have during packing and going through a TSA security check. An interview with a TSA agent also provided a different perspective to the issues that are less aware of.

Experience Problem

In order to improve the experience, it is critical to define the root cause of the problem. To do so, our team used the 5 Whys method, peeling down layers of symptoms to reach the root cause of the problem. We realized that airlines and travel agencies are less involved after travelers book their tickets. Infrequent travelers are not fully informed with the TSA screening process and don’t always check the TSA website for further information.

The 5 Why's

Travelers often feel stressed or stressed at the TSA screening checkpoint

WHY?They are feared of getting their items confiscated or flagged by the officers
WHY?They are not sure with what items are prohibited
WHY?They don't get informed on the TSA guidelines unless they check by themselves
WHY?Booking agencies don't inform their customers
WHY?The service does not include shaping customers' experience before or at the airport​
